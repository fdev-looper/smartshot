SmartShot Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 6, 2025

1. What Data Do We Collect?

Nothing. SmartShot does not collect or transmit any personal data. Your screenshots, download metadata, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

2. How Is Data Stored?

All data is stored securely in Chrome's local storage using the chrome.storage.local API. SmartShot does not transmit data to any server or third-party service.

3. What Permissions Are Used?

SmartShot uses Chrome permissions such as downloads, tabs, and storage to function properly. These are only used for local functionality.

4. Will You Sell or Share My Data?

Nope. We don’t even have access to it. Everything stays on your device. Nothing is sent over the internet.

5. What Happens on Uninstall?

When you uninstall the extension, all local data stored by SmartShot is automatically deleted by your browser.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, reach out at appsbyanuj@gmail.com.
