üìÇ SmartShot File Architecture

SmartShot maintains a structured approach to tracking your downloads:

history.json - The structured data file that stores all your download metadata in a machine-readable format, ensuring your history is preserved even if you reinstall the extension.

history.html - The human-readable dashboard that displays your complete download history in an organized, searchable format, pulling data from the JSON file.

This dual-file approach combines the best of both worlds: structured data storage with a beautiful, user-friendly interface.
