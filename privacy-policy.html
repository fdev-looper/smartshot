SmartShot is designed with privacy at its core. Nothing leaves your computer - ever.

Zero Data Collection: Unlike many screenshot tools, SmartShot doesn't collect, store, or transmit any of your data to external servers. Your screenshots and activity remain exclusively on your device.

Last Updated: August 6, 2025

1. What Data Do We Collect?

Absolutely nothing. SmartShot operates entirely locally on your device. We don't collect screenshots, metadata, usage statistics, or any other information.

2. How Is Data Stored?

All data is stored securely in Chrome's local storage using the chrome.storage.local API. This means:

  • Data never leaves your browser
  • It's encrypted by Chrome's security model
  • Only accessible by you

3. Permission Usage

SmartShot requires standard Chrome permissions (downloads, tabs, storage) solely to provide core functionality. These permissions:

  • Are common for browser extensions
  • Only enable local operations
  • Don't grant any remote access

4. Data Sharing & Selling

We have nothing to share because we don't collect anything in the first place. Our business model doesn't rely on data collection or advertising.

5. Uninstalling SmartShot

When you remove SmartShot, Chrome automatically deletes all associated local data - it's like the extension was never there.

6. Have Questions?

We're transparent about our privacy approach. If you have any concerns, we're happy to explain further.
